GW CS Senior Design 2017

Sean Bird | Faisal Harbi | eVerify

Summary:
Acting as a middleman verification service for customers requesting a loan, and the respective loaning enitites seeking to provide such loan, eVerify bridges this gap by providing an alternative means of verifying financial assets. By utilizing the homomorphic properties of encyrption, eVerify provides its customers the security standards of the CIA Triad (Data Confidentiality, Integrity, and Avalability) during the process of accumulating net financial assets across multiple bank accounts, bonds, or from any other type of financial entity. Ending with a resultant pass or rejection, if a customer meets a satisfactory mutally agreed upon minimum qualifying balance of financial assets to aquire the initial loan.
