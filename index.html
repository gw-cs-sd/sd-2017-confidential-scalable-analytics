GW CS Senior Design 2017

eVERIFY | Faisal Harbi | Sean Bird

Summary:
Acting as a middleman verification service for customers requesting loans, and the respective financial institutions seeking to provide such loans, eVERIFY provides an alternative means of verifying financial assets.

By utilizing the homomorphic properties of encryption, eVERIFY provides its customers the security standards of the CIA Triad (Data Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability) during the process of gathering financial information across multiple bank accounts, bonds, or from any other type of financial asset.

A result of pass or rejection, rather than revealing the customer's total financial asset value, determines whether the customer holds at least the qualifying amount across all owned assets.
