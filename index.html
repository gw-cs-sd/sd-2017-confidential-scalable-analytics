eVerify:
Acting as a middleman verification service for customers requesting a loan, and the respective loaning enitites seeking to provide said loan, eVerify bridges this gap by providing an alternative means of verifying financial assets. By utilizing the homomorphic properties of encyrption, eVerify provides its customers the security standards of the CIA Triad (Data Confidentiality, Integrity, and Avalability) during the proicess of accumulating net financial assets across multiple bank accounts, bonds, or from any other type of financial entity.
